# What makes the cloud atlas encyclopedic?

## Question

What minimum corpus, relationships, and article structure are required for the
application to function as a serious cloud reference rather than a ten-card
introductory overview?

## Working Answer

The corpus must expose the complete formal WMO classification, not only the ten
genera. Genus articles must also explain physical formation, observable
evidence, likely evolution, operational significance, ambiguity, and source
roles. Search and cross-links must let a learner move between a visual clue, a
formal term, and compatible genera.

# Design System

## Selected Direction

**Atlas Swiatla** is the visual truth. It combines a contemporary field atlas,
premium editorial typography, atmospheric photography, warm paper surfaces,
and restrained scientific annotation. The deeper functionality of the
**Pracownia Horyzontu** concept is retained in the information architecture,
diagnostic placement, journal, and expert reference layers.

Reference: `design/reference/atlas-swiatla-mobile.png`

## Personality

Beautiful, observant, calm, exact, and encouraging. The product should feel
like a well-made field atlas that happens to be interactive, not a cockpit,
worksheet, generic dashboard, or children's game.

## Color

- Sky ink: `#082f4f`
- Deep ultramarine: `#064f82`
- Paper: `#f7f2e8`
- Warm white: `#fffdf8`
- Coral annotation: `#be432f`
- Mist blue: `#dcebf0`
- Moss source accent: `#5e7163`
- Graphite body: `#253642`

Colors communicate meaning in addition to decoration. Coral marks the learner's
next action or an observation cue. Moss marks sources and verified reference
material. Blue communicates atmospheric structure.

## Typography

- Display: an open-source editorial serif with Polish glyphs.
- Interface: an open-source humanist sans with Polish glyphs.
- Taxonomy: italic serif for Latin names, never as the only readable label.

Body text stays at 16 px or above on phones. Controls target at least 44 by
44 CSS pixels.

## Layout

- Mobile first at 390 x 844.
- Photography and observation lead; UI supports rather than covers the sky.
- Sections use whitespace, alignment, and dividers before cards or shadows.
- Desktop becomes a study desk with a wider visual stage and persistent
  context, not a stretched phone.

## Motion

Motion explains progression, changing atmospheric levels, and cloud
development. Reduced-motion preferences remove nonessential movement.

## Asset Policy

Generated imagery may be used for decorative hero atmosphere and explanatory
illustration. Identification lessons use real photographs with recorded
license, author, source URL, and subject notes.
